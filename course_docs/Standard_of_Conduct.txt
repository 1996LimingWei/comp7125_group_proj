# Standards of Conduct
## Principle
The University is a community pursuing academic excellence, personal wellness and spiritual maturity. All members shall maintain high honesty and conduct standards. Admitted students must abide by the University’s social, moral and academic regulations throughout study. The University upholds academic freedom, individual rights and open discussion; students shall conduct themselves with dignity and fully understand official publications.

## 1. Core Standards of Conduct
Students must comply with basic norms aligned with the University’s educational purposes and principles:
1. Respect the legitimate rights of others at all times
2. Protect personal and University safety and property
3. Uphold personal integrity and absolute honesty
4. Maintain basic civility and avoid harmful conduct to others
5. Respect all University staff and maintain friendly peer relations
6. Strictly observe all official University rules and regulations

### Typical Misconduct (Non-exhaustive List)
Violations leading to disciplinary action include: academic cheating/plagiarism; disrupting teaching/learning (talking, eating, phone use in class); drunken disorderly behavior; on-campus smoking; illegal drug possession/use; on-campus gambling; theft/damage of personal/University property; unauthorized occupation/damage of campus facilities; document forgery/falsification; indecent behavior; assault/harassment/defamation against staff/students/visitors; refusing ID verification when suspected of rule violations; violating facility usage rules; breaking local laws; hate speech/abusive/threatening language.

## 2. Sanctions for Violations
Offenders will be reviewed by designated officials/panels, with penalties based on severity:
Censure, Written Reprimand, Written Warning, Restitution, Temporary Facility Usage Ban, Mandatory Campus Voluntary Service, Temporary Ban from Student Organization/Committee Positions, Exam Suspension, Academic Probation, Study Suspension, Permanent Dismissal, Other Supplementary Sanctions

## 3. No Smoking Campus Regulation
All indoor and outdoor campus areas are strictly non-smoking zones. Smoking (including e-cigarettes and new tobacco products) is prohibited. Maximum fine for violations: HK$5,000
