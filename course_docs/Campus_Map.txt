# Campus Locations & Transportation
## Kowloon Tong Campus
Comprises 4 sub-campuses: Baptist University Road Campus (BURC), Ho Sin Hang Campus (HSHC), Jockey Club Campus of Creativity (JC3), Shaw Campus (SHAW)

### Transportation
1. MTR
Nearest Station: Kowloon Tong Station
- Walking (10-15 mins): Exit A2 → Pass Suffolk Road → Waterloo Road (Northbound) → Right into Junction Road; HSHC on left, BURC/JC3/SHAW on right
- Minibus: Exit B/D to green minibus terminal; 29A for HSHC/SHAW; 25M/25MS for BURC/JC3

2. Bus Stops & Routes
Kam Shing Road: 2B, 2F, 3C, 7, 85, 85A, 85B, E22
Hong Kong Baptist University: 2B, 2F, 3C, 7, 85, 85A, 85B, 103, 208, E22
Renfrew Road: 103, 208

## Kai Tak Campus
Houses the Academy of Visual Arts; converted from Grade I historic former RAF Officers’ Mess with colonial architectural style

### Transportation
MTR Kwun Tong Line → Choi Hung Station Exit A2 → Pass Tsuen Shek House → Opposite Lam Shek House & Wong Shek House

## Shek Mun Campus
Houses College of International Education & HKBU Affiliated Wong Kam Fai Secondary and Primary School

### Transportation
MTR Ma On Shan Line → Shek Mun Station Exit B (follow directional signs)
