# Master of Science in Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence
Programme Director: Prof LEUNG, Yiu Wing

Programme Overview
The programme focuses on the concepts and practices of information systems and covers three core themes: Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Information Systems. Students acquire theoretical knowledge and hands-on practical skills to solve real-world information system problems.

Upon completion, students will achieve the following learning outcomes:
1. Master the core concepts and methodologies of data analytics, artificial intelligence and intelligent information systems.
2. Apply relevant technologies to conduct problem-solving and practical applications.
3. Identify business needs and transform them into feasible information systems via data and AI technologies.

Study Mode & Duration
1. One-year Full-time: Normal 1 year, maximum 2 years (with approved extension)
2. Two-year Part-time: Normal 2 years, maximum 3 years (with approved extension)
