# Certification of Academic Assessment
## Testimonials
Issued by: Academic Registry (undergraduate & taught postgraduate programmes) / Graduate School (research postgraduate programmes). Purpose: Official proof of student status. Personal recommendation letters should be requested from familiar faculty or staff members.

## Transcripts
Content: Student status, completed courses, grades and GPAs. Official validity: Must bear the designated authorized signature and university official stamp. Fee: Charged for transcript issuance.

## Diplomas
Awarded to graduates who meet all graduation requirements and obtain Senate approval; serves as the official graduation certificate.
