# Master of Science in Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence
Programme Director: Prof LEUNG, Yiu Wing

Programme Introduction
This programme focuses on the concepts and practices of information systems, covering three core themes: Data Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, and Intelligent Information Systems. Students will acquire solid theoretical knowledge and hands-on practical skills to solve real-world information system problems.

Programme Learning Outcomes
Upon completion of the programme, students will be able to:
1. Master core concepts and methodologies of data analytics, artificial intelligence and intelligent information systems
2. Apply relevant technologies to conduct practical problem-solving and application development
3. Identify business demands and transform them into feasible information systems via data and AI technologies

Study Mode & Duration
1. Full-time (1-year): Standard 1 year; maximum 2 years (with official extension approval)
2. Part-time (2-year): Standard 2 years; maximum 3 years (with official extension approval)
