# Academic Dress for Graduates
## 1. Gown
- Doctor (Honoris Causa): Dark red velvet robe, 4.5-inch gold satin front facings trimmed with middle blue satin
- DBA / EdD / PhD: Black robe with royal blue velvet facings and three royal blue velvet sleeve strips
- MPhil / Taught Master: Black robe of Oxford MA pattern
- Postgraduate Diploma: Black robe of Oxford MA pattern
- Bachelor: Simple traditional style black robe

## 2. Hood
- Doctor (Honoris Causa): Dark red velvet hood lined with gold satin
- DBA: Black hood with full blue & gold silk lining, silver grey edge lining + 1cm parallel same-color extra lining
- EdD: Black hood with full blue & gold silk lining, citron edge lining + 1cm parallel same-color extra lining
- PhD: Black hood with full blue & gold silk lining, royal blue velvet edge lining
- MPhil: Black hood with full blue & gold silk lining, deep purple edge lining
- Taught Master: Black hood with full blue & gold silk lining, degree-specific edge lining + 1cm parallel same-degree-color extra lining
- Bachelor: Black hood with full blue & gold silk lining, degree-specific edge lining

## 3. Cap
- Doctor (Honoris Causa): Black velvet bonnet with gold tassel
- DBA: Black bonnet cap with gold tassel
- EdD / PhD: Mortar-board cap with gold tassel
- MPhil / Taught Master / Postgraduate Diploma / Bachelor: Mortar-board cap with black tassel

## 4. Stole
- Postgraduate Diploma: Stole in discipline-specific colour
